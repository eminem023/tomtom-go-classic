How to Update TomTom GO Classic Maps

The TomTom GO Classic is a reliable GPS device built for everyday navigation. To ensure you have the most accurate directions and updated points of interest, regularly updating your device’s maps is crucial.

Why You Should Keep Maps Updated

  • Improves navigation accuracy and reliability
  • Ensures updated speed limits and road signs
  • Adds new routes, POIs, and infrastructure changes
  • Enhances traffic routing and voice guidance

Update Your TomTom GO Classic Using Wi-Fi

  • Turn on your TomTom GO Classic and connect it to Wi-Fi
  • From the main menu, go to Settings → Updates
  • Download and install available map updates
  • Make sure your device remains connected and charged

Update with MyDrive Connect (USB)

  • Download and install MyDrive Connect on your computer
  • Connect your GO Classic using the USB cable
  • Sign in and check for updates
  • Select updates and click Install
  • Do not disconnect during the update process

Fixing Common Update Problems

  • Switch to a different Wi-Fi network if download fails
  • Clear space on the device if memory is full
  • Restart both the GPS device and your PC
  • Reinstall MyDrive Connect if it crashes or stalls

Conclusion

Updating the maps on your TomTom GO Classic ensures you always travel with the latest routing information and navigation data. Follow the simple steps above to keep your GPS smart, responsive, and ready for any journey.
